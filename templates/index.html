History & Origin

Wine has been a symbol of culture, tradition, and celebration for over 8,000 years. It originated in the country of Georgia, where the earliest evidence of wine-making was found. From the ancient Greeks and Romans to today's AI-powered wine recommendations, the journey of wine is rich and evolving.

Modern wine classification uses factors like grape variety, region, aroma, alcohol content, and acidity. With data science, we can now classify wines with precision and even suggest perfect pairings.
